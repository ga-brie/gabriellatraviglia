TIERKREIS.SEGNI DALLO ZODIACO MUSICALE

Gli enti del DVRI suonano le Galassie di Karlheinz Stockhausen

TIERKREIS.SEGNI DALLO ZODIACO MUSICALE has been part of the Galaxies of Culture. Galaxies of Venice programming:Galaxies of Venice: a series of events and workshops inspired by the research project a web application designed to discover and highlight the partnerships and reference themes that unite the cultural and research themes of DVRI - Distretto Veneziano Ricerca e Innovazione.

Starting from the twelve melodies linked to the zodiac signs that make up the Tierkreis of Stockhausen, a workshop has taken shape for the students of Composition and Electronic Music of the Conservatorio Benedetto Marcello, Benedetto Marcello Conservatory, coordinated respectively by Prof. Giovanni Mancuso and Prof. Paolo Zavagna. Each piece has been assigned to one of the bodies that make up the Venetian District of Research and Innovation and performed at the relevant historical venues, revealing the musical essence and references to the cosmos present in the various Venetian palaces but also in the most modern buildings..

The project has led us to play in magnificent palaces such as the Ca' Rezzonico Museum, in the exhibition spaces of the Fondazione Levi Levi Foundation and in the St. Mark's Square Gallery of the Bevilacqua La Masa Institute, as well as in the rooms of the Querini Stampalia Foundation and in other historical settings.

These site-specific performances were recorded in order to create a musical composition involving all DVRI entities. The videos, realized by Cineclub Venezia, tell and show the palaces and the life of the cultural and research realities that participated in the project. A virtual journey on the notes of Stockhausen's Tierkreis to "play in unison" the Galaxies represented by the cultural and research institutions.
