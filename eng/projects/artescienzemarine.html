The Art of Marine Science

The oceans portrayed by artists and researchers

From 11th to 25th October 2019 we opened at the M9 District spaces M9 District Spaces la the exhibition "The Art of Marine Science - The oceans portrayed by artists and researchers" which tells, through the artistic language, the role of the marine environment in our society.

A path born from the collaboration between 24 researchers of the NATIONAL RESEARCH COUNCIL - INSTITUTE OF MARINE SCIENCESand 24 students of the ACCADEMIA DI BELLE ARTI DI VENEZIA, coordinated by DVRI - Distretto Veneziano Ricerca e Innovazione and in collaboration with SCIENCE GALLERY VENICE and M9 DISTRICT.

The works have been realized starting from the scientific articles of the researchers involved: : the exhibition offers the opportunity to undertake a journey between art and science, approaching the latter through the artistic language.

I was curator of this exhibition together with Francesco Falcieri, researcher at CNR ISMAR in Venice.
