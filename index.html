I LOVE

After a degree in Philosophy and a MA in Management of Art and Cultural activities, I have been centralized on Communication and Dissemination of Scientific Research, by gaining an Apprenticeship of Research focused on digital humanities at at Ca’ Foscari University and at DVRI- Distretto Veneziano Ricerca e Innovazione .

In 2017 I joined the Science Gallery Venice Science Gallery Venice team where I work as a community engagement officer managing events, workshops, creative contests and non-formal education programs to bring together communities of students, researchers and artists in a debate on science, technology and art. This position has allowed me to reflect a lot on what fantasy e is and how it can be developed by everyone.

I continued to train in coding and webdesign with a focus on the visual use of image in communication. I would like to collaborate in projects of cultural and social realities or companies dealing with digital communication, media education and innovative use of cultural heritage.
