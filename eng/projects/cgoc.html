Coordinated Galaxies of Venice

Cultural Institutions and Connections in Venice

Coordinated Galaxies of Venice was the final research project I worked on for my research apprenticeship at DVRI and Ca' Foscari University of Venice.

It is an interactive map that allows you to explore collaborative projects between DVRI bodies, revealing their partnerships and common themes.. 8519 projects and 1,037 institutions outside DVRI and with which members collaborated between 2016 and 2019 have been mapped; the projects have been classified into 6 different categories: events, research projects, exhibitions, contests, workshops and publications.

I realized this project together with Alessando Descovi, Ceo and web-developer at Rubynetti

The following tools were used to create the app: Ruby on Rails and Vue js ed3.js.
