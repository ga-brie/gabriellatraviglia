Beni Relazionali

Una mappa per esplorare le relazioni solidali

As a research fellow in the project INTEGRATED SUSTAINABILITY AND SOCIAL INNOVATION, promoted by Università Cà Foscari, ISRE and CISL, I was in charge of designing and developing the dissemination portal on which to display data. The final product was developed together with Alessandro Descovi, CEO of Rubynetti.

I worked closely with an interdisciplinary working group that aimed to examine the concept of Relational Good within the perimeter of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

The concept of relational good, which appeared in the scientific debate at an interdisciplinary level since the mid 1980s, describes an immaterial good (Gui), very important for "human blossoming" (Nussbaum), which coincides with a non-instrumental nature relationship between non-arbitrary people (Uhlaner) that emerges (Donati) from their interaction with each other according to a predominantly unconditional logic, i.e. not guided by the certainty of what they will have in return, as happens on the contrary in contractual relationships.

HI collaborated in data collection, devising a data collection form in agreement with the research team, which was subsequently implemented by Rubynetti. The tools used for the implementation were Rails and Bootstrap, which have an accessible and user-friendly interface.

The data collection involved about 30 agricultural production realities of the Veneto region coming from OltreConfin – Distretto di Economia Solidale, a group of organic producers and the District of Agromania.

Finally, the project ended with the creation of the interface on which to visualize the data collected and explore the relational assets present in the territory.

For this section more experimental technologies and tools have been used as there are ready or standardized solutions. The interactive mapping of the interface has been developed in rails with a small sub app developed in Vue.js and D3.js.
