TIERKREIS.SEGNI DALLO ZODIACO MUSICALE.

I curated together with Giovanni Mancuso, Paolo Zavagna and Alice Fasano a cycle of site-specific performances that gave life to the project Tierkreis. Segni Dallo Zodiaco Musicale. In collaboration with Benedetto Marcello Conservatory of Venice, the project is inspired by the twelve melodies linked to the zodiac signs that compose Stockhausen's Tierkreis, which are played in some of the most incredible places in Venice: Ca' Rezzonico, Palazzo Pisani, the Arsenale Nord and the Querini Stampalia Foundation.
