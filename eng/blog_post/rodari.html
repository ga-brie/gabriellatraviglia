Con letture di tre racconti di Gianni Rodari

On October 23, 1920 Gianni Rodari was born in Omegna, in the province of Novara. Gianni Rodari was a great author for children and teenagers and in 1970 he was awarded what is called the "little nobel", the nobel prize for children's literature, namely the Andersen prize.

Gianni Rodari was the first Italian author to receive this award. His literary production is about seventy works among nursery rhymes, poems, short stories, novels, theatrical texts. These works, however, have been composed and decomposed, reorganized, modified through different curators and for different school editions. This process of unification and re-unification of his works continued even after his death, mainly with respect to his writings that appeared on the pioneer and the children's courier.

But it cannot be said that he was just a writer: Gianni Rodari was a journalist.
