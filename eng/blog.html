School and Cultural Institutions

An article of mine published in the Menabò di Etica e Economia on the relationship between school and institutions.
In this article, after recalling the regulatory framework on the relationship between school and cultural institutions, I argue that the COVID emergency pushes us to imagine practices of acting to create a concrete alliance between digital, culture and education and that appropriating physical spaces, for example by making school at the museum, as well as virtual spaces, developing digital projects based on cooperation with cultural institutions, would make young people aware of how important it is to regain shared ownership of cultural heritage.
